Best Free Games for Low-End PCs in 2025

May 2025 ⏱ 7 min read Gaming

You don't need a powerful rig to have fun. These free-to-play games run smoothly on low-end PCs and laptops — including machines with integrated graphics and 4GB of RAM. We tested all of them on budget hardware.

Our Top Picks

1. Valorant Very Low Specs

Riot's tactical shooter is one of the best optimized games ever made. It runs at 60+ FPS on almost any PC made after 2015. The gameplay is deep, competitive, and completely free.

Min RAM: 4GB Min GPU: Intel HD 4000 Storage: 22GB

Why it's great: Valorant has one of the most active competitive scenes and regular content updates. Even on a potato PC you can hit high ranks with good aim and game sense.

2. League of Legends Very Low Specs

The most played PC game in the world runs on nearly anything. If your PC can browse the web, it can run League. Hundreds of hours of gameplay, completely free.

Min RAM: 2GB Min GPU: Intel HD 3000 Storage: 16GB

The learning curve is steep but the game rewards mastery. With 160+ champions to choose from, you'll never run out of new things to try.

3. Fortnite Low-Medium Specs

Fortnite has a Performance Mode specifically designed for low-end PCs, dropping graphics to minimal but keeping the game playable at 60 FPS on older hardware.

Min RAM: 4GB Min GPU: Intel HD 4000 Storage: 30GB

Enable Performance Mode in settings to get the most FPS out of your hardware. It makes the game look simpler but runs dramatically smoother.

💡 Low-End PC Tip Always check a game's minimum requirements before downloading. Sites like SystemRequirementsLab.com let you test your PC specs against any game's requirements for free.
4. Path of Exile Low-Medium Specs

One of the deepest free-to-play ARPGs ever made. Path of Exile offers hundreds of hours of content with an insane skill tree and build variety — all completely free.

Min RAM: 4GB Min GPU: ATI X1900 / NVIDIA 8800 Storage: 40GB

Lower the graphics settings in options and it runs fine on budget hardware. The free-to-play model is also genuinely fair — no pay-to-win at all.

5. Genshin Impact Low-Medium Specs

A gorgeous open-world RPG that's surprisingly playable on low-end PCs at minimum settings. The game has an enormous amount of content and a generous free-to-play model.

Min RAM: 8GB Min GPU: NVIDIA GT 1030 Storage: 30GB

Set graphics to "Lowest" and resolution to 720p for smooth gameplay. The story and world exploration are excellent even at low visual settings.

6. Warframe Very Low Specs

A fast-paced action game with space ninjas. Warframe has an absurd amount of content for a free game and runs well on older hardware. The community is also one of the friendliest in gaming.

Min RAM: 4GB Min GPU: DirectX 10 Storage: 35GB
7. CS2 (Counter-Strike 2) Low-Medium Specs

The legendary tactical shooter is free on Steam. CS2 replaced CS:GO and runs well on budget hardware when graphics are lowered. One of the most skill-rewarding games ever made.

Min RAM: 8GB Min GPU: GTX 970 recommended (runs on less) Storage: 30GB

Use launch options like -high -novid -nojoy to squeeze extra performance out of your hardware.

Bottom Line

All of these games offer hundreds of hours of entertainment without costing you a cent. Start with Valorant or League of Legends if you want competitive games, Path of Exile if you love deep RPGs, or Warframe if you want co-op action.

The best part? You can try all of them for free and keep the ones you enjoy. No commitment required.
