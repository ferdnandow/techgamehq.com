Best Gaming Mice Under $30 in 2025

April 2025 ⏱ 6 min read Reviews

You don't need to spend $100+ on a gaming mouse. In 2025, budget mice have caught up significantly in sensor quality and build. These picks under $30 will perform well in any game.

~$25 on Amazon Best Value

The G203 is the go-to recommendation for budget gaming mice. It uses an excellent optical sensor, has a comfortable ambidextrous shape, and Logitech's build quality is outstanding at this price point.

💡 What to Look for in a Budget Gaming Mouse Focus on sensor quality (optical beats laser), polling rate (1000Hz is standard), and weight. RGB lighting is nice but irrelevant to performance. Most budget mice now use sensors that were flagship-grade just 3 years ago.
4. Corsair Katar Pro ~$25

Ultra-light at just 73g, the Katar Pro is one of the lightest mice at this price. Great for claw grip players and those who prefer a smaller, faster mouse.

12,400 DPI 1ms polling 73g weight Ambidextrous

Our Pick: Logitech G203

For most gamers, the Logitech G203 is the easy recommendation. The sensor is excellent, the shape works for most hand sizes, and Logitech's build quality means it'll last years. At around $25, it's hard to beat.

If you're right-handed and prefer a larger palm grip mouse, the Razer DeathAdder Essential is the better choice. For lighter-weight preference, go with the SteelSeries Rival 3 or Corsair Katar Pro.
