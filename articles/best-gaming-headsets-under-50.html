Best Gaming Headsets Under $50 in 2025

May 2025 ⏱ 6 min read Reviews

You don't need to spend $150 on a gaming headset. These picks under $50 deliver solid sound, clear mics, and comfortable wear for long sessions — without destroying your wallet.

Our Top Picks

1. HyperX Cloud Stinger 2 Best Overall
~$40

The Cloud Stinger 2 is the go-to recommendation for budget gaming headsets. HyperX knows how to make comfortable headsets and the Stinger 2 is no exception — lightweight, well-padded, and easy to wear for hours.

2. Logitech G335 ~$50

Logitech's budget entry is surprisingly good. The G335 is extremely lightweight at just 240g, making it one of the most comfortable headsets at any price. Sound quality is clear and the mic is better than most competitors at this price.

  • Works on all platforms

3. Razer BlackShark V2 X ~$40

The BlackShark V2 X punches above its weight in sound quality. The 50mm drivers deliver a soundstage that rivals more expensive headsets. The cardioid mic is also better than most budget options.

Cons

💡 Gaming Headset vs Studio Headphones If sound quality matters more to you than a built-in mic, consider getting studio headphones (like the Audio-Technica ATH-M20x at ~$50) and a separate clip-on mic. You'll get significantly better audio for the same price.

Our Pick

For most gamers, the HyperX Cloud Stinger 2 is the best choice — comfortable for long sessions, solid sound, and a reliable mic. If sound quality is your priority, stretch slightly to the Razer BlackShark V2 X for noticeably better audio.

All three are available on Amazon and frequently go on sale. Check during Prime Day and Black Friday for deals up to 40% off.
